Pizza Dough

Pizza dough is so easy to make it only takes a few minutes. STOP BUYING PREMADE DOUGHT

Ingrediants (10in dough):

  • All-Purpose Flour/Bread Flour(2 1/3cups)
  • Instant Yeast(2 1/4ts)
  • Sugar(1 1/2ts)
  • Salt(3/4ts)
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil(2tbs)
  • Water(3/4cup)
