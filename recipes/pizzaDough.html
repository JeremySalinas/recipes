Pizza Dough

Pizza dough is so easy to make it only takes a few minutes. STOP BUYING PREMADE DOUGHT

Ingrediants (10in dough):

  • All-Purpose Flour/Bread Flour(2 1/3cups)
  • Instant Yeast(2 1/4ts)
  • Sugar(1 1/2ts)
  • Salt(3/4ts)
  • Olive Oil(2tbs)
  • Water(3/4cup)

Directions:

  1. Get all the dry ingrediants(flour/yeast/sugar/salt) and pour them into a bowl
  2. Mix all the dry ingrediants for about 5 minutes
  3. Add 2tbs of olive oil and 3/4 cup of warm Water
  4. Mix and keep adding flour until it doesn't stick to your hands
  5. Get another bowl and cover it with olive oil so it doesn't stick
  6. Place dough into the new bowl and wrap the bowl so no air gets in
  7. Wait for 30 minutes
  8. Preheat oven to 400F
  9. Place sauce and toppings of your choice and enjoy!
