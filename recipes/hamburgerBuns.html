Hamburger Buns

Stop buying those mid af buns in the Supermarket and start making these bomb buns

Ingrediants (9 buns):

  • Bread Flower(3 1/2cups)
  • Vegatble Oil(2tbs)
  • Sugar(2ts)
  • Salt(1 1/2ts)
  • Milk(1/2cup)
  • Water(1/2cup)
  • Yeast(2 1/2ts)
  • Eggs(3)

Directions:

  1. Pour 1/2 cup of water and milk together
  2. Add 2 1/2 teaspoon of yeast.
  3. Mix and let it rest (10min)
  4. Add 3 1/2 cup of bread flour , 2 teaspoon of suger, 1 1/2 teaspoon salt, then mix
  5. While mixing add 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil, the yeast, one egg, and one egg yolk
  6. Mix for about 5 minutes
  7. Let it rest in greased bowl for 1 hour
  8. Divide 9 even pieces and roll into ball
  9. Brush ball with beaten egg
  10. Pre cook oven to 375Â°
  11. Cook till golden brown (20ish minutes)
