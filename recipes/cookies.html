Cookie Recipe

If for some reason your gradma didnt have a cookie recipe(like mine) then this will be your grandma recipe

Ingrediants (6 big cookies):

  • Sugar(1/2cup)
  • Brown Sugar(3/4cup)
  • Salt(1ts)
  • Unsalted Butter(stick)
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Extract(1ts)
  • All-Purpose Flour(1 1/4cups)
  • Baking Soda(1/2ts)
  • Chocolate(4oz)

Directions:

  1. Mix 1/2cup of sugar, 3/4cup of brown sugar, and 1ts salt
  2. Melt the stick of butter over medium heat
  3. Pour the melted butter into the bowl with mixed sugar/salt and mix
  4. Add the egg, 1ts vanilla extract, 1/2ts baking soda, and mix
  5. Add 1 1/4cup of flour and mix
  6. Cut the Choclate into small pieces then add and mix
  7. Preheat oven to 375F
  8. Place on a tray and into the oven
  9. Place timer to 10 minutes
  10. Take out cookies, wait for about 5 minutes to cool
  11. Enjoy!
